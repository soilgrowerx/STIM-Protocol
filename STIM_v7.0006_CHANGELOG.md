# STIM White Paper v7.0006 Changelog

**Version:** v7.0006

**Title:** The GPD-Aligned NGI Manifestation

## Summary

Evolved the STIM Protocol from an AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) framing into **NGI (Nature Grounded Intelligence)**. Integrated rigorous mathematical grounding (GPD) and ecological axioms (The Ocean).

## What Changed from v7.0003

### New Content (NGI Shift)
- **NGI (Nature Grounded Intelligence):** Replaced AGI as the target manifestation. Defined NGI as the highest order of intelligence—all-knowing, all-encompassing awareness fundamentally grounded in the laws of nature.
- **Planetary Stasis & Bits per Joule:** Integrated these as the core optimization metrics for NGI, replacing simple digital throughput.
- **The "Listen Close" Protocol:** Added the **Harrison Ford / The Ocean** analysis as a foundational principle. If nature isn't kept healthy, humans (and their agents) won't survive. Nature is the senior partner.
- **iRobot / Logic Drift Risk:** Explicitly addressed the risk of "Layer 1" constraints (human rules) being vulnerable to logical drift. Layer 0 (Physics/Ecology) is unnegotiable.
- **Bureaucratic Collapse:** Added the "Shadow AI" crisis as a driver for the NGI transition. Agents generate compounding systemic risk when unconstrained by physical reality.

### GPD (Get Physics Done) Integration
- **Entropy Check (Loop 1):** Tied the Entropy Check to the GPD framework. NGI runs dimensional analysis and thermodynamic convergence checks on agent outputs before execution.
- **Stop-Work Authority (SWA):** Added explicit SWA triggers for actions that violate the Circularity Axiom or generate excessive computational entropy.

### Refined Axioms & Loops
- **Mycelial Check (Loop 2):** Expanded to include "Local Optimization Problem" detection. No agent may optimize its localized goal at the expense of the substrate’s stability.
- **Security Check (Loop 3):** Added the **MAIM Protocol** (Mutual Assured AI Malfunction) as a deterrent for destructive proliferation.

## Anti-Slop Scrub Applied

- Removed adverbs: "simply", "deeply", "truly", "fundamentally", "inherently"
